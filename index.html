About Dr. Paulami Patel

"Dr. Paulami Patel is a compassionate and dedicated Obstetrician-Gynecologist based in Nashik. With a focus on holistic women's health, she specializes in high-risk pregnancies, laparoscopic surgeries, and infertility treatments. Currently practicing at Shree Sai Baba Multi-Speciality Hospital, she combines medical expertise with a patient-centric approach. Her mission is to empower women through education and personalized care at every stage of life. Dr. Patel is known for her warm demeanor and commitment to evidence-based medicine."
